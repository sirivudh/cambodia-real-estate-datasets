# Cambodia Real Estate Data

This site provides curated datasets and supporting documentation related to
Cambodia’s real estate market. It consolidates public, redistributed, and
aggregated data to support market research, analysis, and long-term insight
into residential property trends.

The project is maintained as an evolving data resource and will expand over
time as new market indicators and datasets become available.

---

## Available Datasets

### Residential Property Price Indices
Structured datasets tracking residential price movements over time, based on
officially published indices and consolidated into analysis-ready formats.

These datasets are intended for trend analysis and market research rather than
regulatory or valuation use.

---

### Condominium Supply & Completion Data
Datasets documenting completed and handed-over condominium projects, with a
focus on major urban areas such as Phnom Penh.

Coverage includes:
- Completion or handover period
- Location or district
- Approximate unit counts
- High-level price range classifications

---

## Data Principles

- Data is provided for research and informational purposes
- Original sources are preserved where applicable
- Methodology and metadata are referenced alongside the datasets
- No claim of official or regulatory authority is made

Users should always consult original publications or primary sources when
using the data for formal analysis or decision-making.
